Questionaire (Quantitative)

A survey was sent out that asked what players thought about Elite: Dangerous's UI. The questions in the survery were based on the System Usability Scale(SUS) which can be found HERE.

One issue with this survey was that the sample size was small, it only had 5 responces. Another issue with the survey was that the questions were very broad and answers for the specific parts of the interface could be pretty different.

From the results of the survey, most of the users thought the interface was immersive and not very cumbersome to use. Furthermore everyone who answerd the survey said that there was very little inconsistency in the UI.

UI Strengths UI Weaknesses
